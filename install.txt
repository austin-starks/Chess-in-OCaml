To play the game, run make play. The instructions are on the screen. In summary:

- Use "stalemate" to draw the game

- Use "score" to calculate the score of each player

- Otherwise, use two positions to indicate which pieces you want to move. For example, 
if you want to move your pawn above your king, and you are white, you input e2 e4 
to indicate you want to move the pawn at position E2 to position E4
